## List your business in three simple steps

Join the ShopLocal community and start reaching customers in your area.

### Step 1: Create your business account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Sign up for ShopLocal">
    Visit [shoplocal.online/signup](https://shoplocal.online/signup) and create your free business account.
    
    You'll need:
    - Business name and contact information
    - Business address and operating hours
    - A valid email address for verification
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="building" title="Complete your business profile">
    1. Add your business details and description
    2. Upload your logo and photos
    3. Set your business categories and services
    4. Add your contact information and social media links
    
    <Tip>Businesses with complete profiles get 3x more customer views!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Customize your storefront

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="palette" title="Brand your business page">
    Make your ShopLocal page uniquely yours:
    
    1. Choose your brand colors and theme
    2. Add your business story and what makes you special
    3. Upload high-quality photos of your products or location
    4. Add special offers or promotions
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="clock" title="Set your availability">
    Keep customers informed about when to visit:

    1. Add your regular business hours
    2. Mark special hours for holidays
    3. Enable real-time status updates (Open/Closed)
    4. Set appointment or reservation options if applicable

    <Tip>Accurate hours reduce customer frustration and increase visits!</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Go live and grow

<Accordion icon="rocket" title="Launch your business on ShopLocal">
  1. Review your profile for accuracy
  2. Click "Publish" to make your business discoverable
  3. Share your ShopLocal page on social media
  4. Start connecting with local customers!
</Accordion>
